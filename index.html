During my 10 years as a photographer I worked for many companies and people, capturing images of everything from births to deaths.
I primarily used Canon cameras but one of my favorites was the Mamiya RZ67. A beautiful medium format film camera made by Mamiya from 1982 until 2004.

One of my favorite projects I started was Social Media project titled Humans of Seattle.
The project was based around photographing and interviewing people I found interesting or intriguing in the streets of Seattle, Wa.
